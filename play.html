Personal Interests

Storytelling

Stories have always captured my interest. In the past, I wished my brain would wander to data structures and algorithms, but stories ended up becoming even more useful.

I tell stories to build empathy for customers. Stories are a perfect fit for life as a Product Manager. They help me illustrate customer needs and show where customers are challenged by new products.

In particular, I like animated stories. All kinds of films interest me, whether they are fictional or real, stop-motion or digital, Japanese or American. My favorite studios are Lycra, Ghibli, and Pixar. Animators whom I admire are Hayao Miyazaki and John Lasseter.
